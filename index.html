About

University of Warwick

I am a current undergraduate at the University of Warwick studying Computer Science. My academic interests include functional programming, cyber security, web development and artificial intelligence. I am also an experienced tutor with a demonstrated history of working in the e-learning industry. I am a skilled programmer in Haskell, Python, Java, C, C# and SQL with experience in software engineering, database design, low and high level programming, network design and penetration testing. Please see my github for personal projects.

My dissertation is entitled "Hale: a web based tool to teach functional programming". The project is to design a back-end compiler and front-end teaching tool to teach 'Hale'. Hale is a custom purely functional, statically typed, programming language I have developed. it is most similar to Lambda Calculus but with the inclusion of primitives and types. My supervisor is Jonathon Foss.

Please see my GitHub for my personal projects.
